Our Services

Guidance and Counseling: Our dedicated guidance counselors are available to assist students with academic planning, college and career guidance, personal development, and emotional support. We offer both individual and group counseling sessions.

Special Education Services: We provide tailored support for students with special needs, including individualized education plans (IEPs), specialized instruction, and access to resources to ensure every student reaches their full potential.

Health and Wellness: Our school health services include a fully-equipped clinic staffed by qualified healthcare professionals. We offer regular health check-ups, vaccinations, and wellness programs to promote a healthy school community.

Library and Media Center: Our library is a hub of learning and exploration, offering a vast collection of books, digital resources, and research tools. We also provide media center services, including computer access, printing, and multimedia equipment.

Student Support Services: To help students succeed academically, we offer tutoring programs, study skills workshops, and homework assistance. Our teachers and staff are committed to providing personalized support and enrichment opportunities.

Parental Engagement: We believe in fostering strong partnerships with parents and guardians. Our school offers various programs and workshops to keep parents informed and involved in their child's education. We also host regular parent-teacher meetings and family events.

Community Outreach: Sta. Lucia High School actively engages with the local community through various outreach programs and volunteer opportunities. We encourage students to participate in community service projects to develop a sense of civic responsibility and leadership.

Transportation Services: For the convenience of our students and families, we provide safe and reliable transportation services. Our school buses are equipped with modern safety features and operated by trained drivers.

Technology Integration: We embrace technology to enhance learning and streamline school operations. Our services include access to online learning platforms, digital classrooms, and technical support for students and staff.
